about yobel

people

Ethical treatment of people is our number one priority. We only carry products manufactured by people who are paid a fair, livable wage and have safe working conditions. We partner with about 60 global artisans and companies committed to the same standards.

planet

To be better stewards of our incredible planet, we carry products from companies and artisans committed to sustainable practices. This means impact to the environment is minimized from sourcing materials to manufacturing and shipping.

profit

We believe that profit doesn't have to come from exploiting people or our planet's resources. Profit is about a mutually beneficial relationship between the people we work with and the planet we live on. When people and our planet are priority, we all profit.
